The Journey of Our Founder

Joseph, the founder of Joseph Orphanage Home, began his journey in a small town in Uromi. Growing up with little but a heart full of love, he learned that the greatest gift he could give was hope. After completing his studies at Ilaro Polytechnic and working briefly in the construction industry, Joseph realized his true calling wasn't in building buildings, but in building lives.

In 2017, with little more than a dream and the will to make a difference, Joseph founded Joseph Orphanage Home. His vision was simple: provide vulnerable children with a loving, nurturing home, where they could find safety, education, and the opportunity to dream. What started with just a few children has grown into a beacon of hope for many.

About Us

Joseph Orphanage Home was founded with the belief that every child deserves a chance at a better life. Located in Uromi, Nigeria, our orphanage is dedicated to providing a safe and loving home for abandoned, orphaned, and vulnerable children. Under the guidance of Joseph Omoregie, we offer a family-like environment where children receive care, education, and emotional support.

Our mission is to empower each child to grow into a self-sufficient, compassionate individual through education, skills training, and personal development. With the generous support of our donors and partners, we are creating a future full of opportunities for the children in our care.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission

At Joseph Orphanage Home, our mission is to provide a safe, loving, and nurturing environment for orphaned and vulnerable children. We aim to offer each child the opportunity to thrive through education, life skills, and emotional support, preparing them for a bright and independent future.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading light for orphaned and disadvantaged children across Nigeria. We strive to create a future where every child has access to education, love, and the opportunity to succeed, no matter their background.

Values

  • Compassion: We believe in the power of love and empathy to heal and transform.
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethical responsibility in all that we do.
  • Empowerment: We equip children with education, skills, and emotional support to become confident individuals.
  • Community: We foster collaboration with individuals and organizations to strengthen the well-being of our children.
  • Respect: Every child is valued and treated with dignity and respect.
  • Sustainability: We ensure that our efforts contribute to the long-term well-being and growth of our children.
